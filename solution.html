Cybersecurity Maturity Assessment Approach

  • Maturity ratings: Assessment of current profiles in layers for implementation on a scale of 1 to 4. This is applied to every NIST CSF category question depending on the interviews with key stakeholders and IT professionals.
  • Scoring: Maturity score calculations are used to assess the maturity levels of a NIST CSF security category. It also aids in testing overall functions dependent on the ratings and comparative analysis
  • Risk and impact statement: enunciates the importance of security in an organization and proves how security deficiency can have an impact
  • Comparative analysis: evaluate the organization’s current profile and the required target profile against relevant industrial peers. Ampcus Cyber maintains a database of these evaluations and uses contemporary industry data sets for peer comparisons.
  • Strategic program recommendations: provide security drives and initiates programs that aid in increasing the company’s overall security demeanor. Also allows exceptional management of risk exposure in compliance with the organization’s risk capacity.
